Evidence System

How users see and feel their progress ‚Äî proof against imposter syndrome

UX Architecture Brief ‚Ä¢ v1.0

Transformation without proof feels like imagination.
Evidence makes change real.

The Evidence System captures, displays, and celebrates progress throughout the 90-day journey. It's the antidote to "nothing's working" and the proof users need when imposter syndrome strikes.

Section 1 ‚Äî Why Evidence Matters

Internal transformation is invisible. Users often can't see their own growth because it happens gradually. Without evidence, they discount progress and quit ‚Äî or finish and forget how far they came.

The Evidence Principle: What gets measured gets managed. What gets celebrated gets repeated. The Evidence System does both ‚Äî it tracks progress and makes progress feel good.

Without Evidence

"I've been doing this for 30 days and I don't feel any different."

"I don't know if this is actually working."

"Maybe I'm just not cut out for this."

‚Üí Quits at Day 45

With Evidence

"I've logged 23 wins. I ran the Fear Interrupt 6 times. I finished Sprint 3."

"I can see exactly where I was on Day 1 vs. now."

"The proof is right here. I'm different."

‚Üí Finishes and keeps going

Section 7 ‚Äî Before/After Capture

The most powerful evidence is the contrast between Day 1 and Day 90. We capture this explicitly.

Day 1 Capture (Onboarding)

During Creative Archaeology, we capture:

Creative dormancy duration
Primary blockers identified
Current emotional state
What they want to change
Biggest fear about trying

Day 90 Capture (Completion)

During Completion Ceremony, we capture:

Current creative state
Blockers overcome
Biggest transformation
What they built/started/changed
Message to Day 1 self

The Before/After Artifact: At completion, users see their Day 1 answers alongside their Day 90 answers. This is often an emotional moment ‚Äî concrete proof they're different. This artifact becomes their testimonial (with permission) and their personal proof for life.
